Kottayam: Latex, Lakes and Letters

The city is an important trading center of spices and commercial crops, especially rubber. Most of India’s natural rubber originates from the acres of well-kept plantations of Kottayam. Kottayam is also known as the “city of letters” considering its contribution to print media and literature.

Places to visit

Marmala Waterfalls

Come experience a hidden treasure! Marmala Waterfalls are popularly known as the ‘enchantress of the jungle’. Slightly hidden amidst thick bushes and blue hills, the location can only be reached by a short trek on the hillside. The waterfalls is the perfect get-away from the chaos of the city

Taj Juma Masjid

One of the oldest mosques in India and more than 1000 years old. It is famous for its intricate architecture and wood carvings. This mosque is situated on the banks of the Meenachil river.

Vaikom

Surrounded by the Vembanad Lake, this lakeside town attracts with its beautiful water bodies and serene village life. Explore the backwaters, narrow canals and life of the small villages of Vaikom through canoe cruises.
