Kannur: Crown of Kerala

Since ages, Kannur been hailed as an influential sea port. The area had interactions and trade relations with the rest of the world since the age of King Solomon to the Greeks, Arabs and Romans. Numerous forts, temples, beaches and wildlife sanctuaries await your visit. Major festivals and events are held throughout the year and this is among the best places in Kerala to spend your vacation in.

St. Angelo Fort

A historic fort in Kannur built with red laterite stones, it rests on a cliff situated beside the majestic Arabian Sea and is surrounded by a beautiful garden. The fort has huge chapels, stables, a prison and an ammunition storehouse. The best time to visit the fort is during an early part of the day or during sunset.

Vismaya Water Park

Cool off at this water themed park replete with a plethora of amusing rides and thrilling water sports. The amazing decor complements the cosy infrastructure. This is a great place to spend time with family and friends.
