Kozhikode: City of Spices

A major trading point for Indian spices and a once famous cotton-weaving centre. This is a beautiful coastal town with with the vast Arabian Sea on the west and the peaks of Wayanad hill on the east. Its believed that Vasco-da-Gama first set foot in India at Kozhikode when the Spice Route was established, hence the name, City of Spices. For history lovers, Calicut is the perfect place to be.

Places to visit

Mananchira Square

Located in the heart of Kozhikode city, Mananchira Square is a beautiful park with lush green lawns, fascinating sculptures, Kerala-style buildings, an open-air theatre and a musical fountain. The park is built around the man-made Mananchira Lake. It offers good opportunities for picnics and pleasant walks.

Kozhikode Beach

Come and enjoy the sunsets from this beach and a fresh supply of seafood. Mussels are a local favourite here. A walk to Dolphins Point at dawn ensures a meeting with playful dolphins. You are also greeted by a majestic lighthouse and two piers opening into the sea. Both have been here for over a hundred years. For children, the beach offers the Lions Park and the nearby marine water aquarium.

HiLite Mall

HiLite Mall is the largest mall of North Kerala. Second largest in the state and amongst the biggest malls in the country. The arcade consists of over 200 shopping outlets and numerous eateries. An expansive gaming zone within the premises is the major attraction among the kids. The area has a bowling zone, dashing cars, dark house, video games among many others.
