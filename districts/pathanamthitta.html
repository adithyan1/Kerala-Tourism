Pathanamthitta

Pathanamthitta is one of the most important pilgrim centers in Kerala with many Temples and Churchs. Sabarimala, a famous pilgrim center, and Maramon, where the world famous Maramon convention is conducted, are in this district. The world famous Aranmula mirrors comes from here. The elephant-training center at Konni is another attraction of the district. More than fifty per cent of this district is covered by forests.

Places to visit

Gavi

Gavi, located in Pathanamthitta district offers its visitors activities like trekking, wildlife watching, outdoor camping in specially built tents, and night safaris. Gavi is listed among the leading eco-tourism centres and one of the must-see places in India.

Sabarimala

A temple complex located at Sabarimala hill inside the Periyar Tiger Reserve in the Perinad Village, Pathanamthitta district, Kerala, India. It is one of the largest annual pilgrimage sites in the world. The temple is situated on a hilltop amidst eighteen hills at an altitude of 1260 m.

Perunthenaruvi Waterfall

A popular place in Kerala to relax, and rejuvenate, Perunthenaruvi waterfall by the bank of the Pamba River against the backdrop of the Western ghats is a treat to eyes. The waterfall got its name from the two Malayalam words - Perunthu (great honey) and aruvi (stream). Best time to see it in its full glory has to be the monsoon.
